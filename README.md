## 📊 Project Overview

This project presents an interactive Power BI dashboard developed to analyze electric vehicle charging behavior, charging station performance, and key operational metrics.

The dashboard transforms raw charging session data into actionable business insights, helping stakeholders understand usage patterns, revenue drivers, charger utilization, and customer charging behavior.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

The dashboard was designed to answer the following business questions:

- Which charging station locations generate the highest revenue?
- When do customers charge their vehicles most frequently?
- Which charger types deliver the most energy?
- Which vehicle models consume the most energy?
- How does charging behavior vary throughout the day and week?

---

## 🚀 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

| KPI | Description |
|------|-------------|
| Total Revenue | Total revenue generated from charging sessions |
| Total Energy Delivered | Total energy consumed (kWh) |
| Total Charging Sessions | Number of charging sessions |
| Average Session Duration | Average charging duration in hours |
| Average Charging Cost | Average cost per charging session |

---

## 📈 Dashboard Features

### Revenue by Location
Analyze revenue generated across charging station locations.

### Charging Sessions by Day of Week
Identify weekly charging demand patterns and usage behavior.

### Revenue by Time of Day
Discover peak charging periods and customer charging habits.

### Energy Consumption by Charger Type
Compare charger utilization and energy delivery across charging technologies.

### Energy Consumption by Vehicle Model
Analyze charging demand across different electric vehicle models.

## 🔍 Key Insights

- Revenue varies across charging station locations.
- Evening charging sessions generate the highest revenue.
- Different charger types contribute differently to total energy delivery.
- Vehicle models show distinct charging and energy consumption patterns.
- Charging demand changes throughout the week and across time periods.
